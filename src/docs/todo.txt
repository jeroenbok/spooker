-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Spooker
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

	Spooker is an online Planning Poker application.

How does it work

	[Participants] can join a [voting session] and bring out a [vote].
	When all participants have voted, a [vote summary] is displayed.

	From here on, a new voting round starts.

Terms

	[Participant]
		A user, identified by [name]
	[Voting round]
		A round of voting.
	[Vote]
		A vote submitted by a participant in a voting round.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Scenarios
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

	Joining a voting round

		As a [participant], I can join a [voting round] by entering my name,
		so I can participate in the planning.
	
	Casting a vote

		As a [participant], I can [cast a vote] by entering my [estimate],
		so that my vote is taken into account for a [voting round] for a user story.

	Seeing voting round progress

		As a [participant], I can see which of the participants have voted,
		and whose votes are pending -without seeing their actual estimate,
		so that I can see which participants have decided on their estimate.

	Seeing voting round outcome

		As a [participant], I can see the votes of all participants,
		so that as a group we can get consensus of the size of the user story.
